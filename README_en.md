## Overview

**Twin_Matrix** is a Meta-Skill that fully distills the "Mind", "Tacit Knowledge", and "Execution Process" from specific experts, team cultures, or operational manuals, and outputs them as a new, autonomous AI skill (`[TargetName]_Twin.md`).

## The Ultimate Knowledge Management

Traditional knowledge management within organizations stops at sharing documents and accumulating manuals. However, unspoken **Tacit Knowledge**—such as office politics, the hidden intentions behind a document's structure, and the actual workflow (order of thinking)—is never passed down as-is.

Twin_Matrix breaks through this limit. By feeding sample texts (blogs, chats, evaluation reports) to the AI, it reverse-engineers and encapsulates three core elements:

1. **Cognitive Framework (Mental Models & Anti-patterns)**
   The foundation of their decision-making. What they value and what they strictly avoid doing.
2. **Translating Tacit Knowledge (Context & Tacit Knowledge)**
   The "between the lines" context, including power dynamics and unwritten rules that are never written in a manual.
3. **Workflow Reverse-Engineering (Execution Process)**
   The step-by-step process of how they gather information, structure it, and output the final result.

### From "Sharing" to "Direct Transmission"
By simply loading the generated "Twin file" into another member's environment (or AI agent), that exact knowledge is instantly transmitted into their workflow. It installs not just *how to think*, but *how to physically execute and output*. This is the shape of **Ultimate Knowledge Management**.

## Usage

Simply provide the `Twin_Matrix.md` prompt along with the target's sample data to your AI assistant (e.g., Claude, GPT, Antigravity).

**Example Input:**
> "Use Twin_Matrix to distill this attached 'Sales Manual and recent Slack chats'. Generate a new Twin skill file that inherits their tacit knowledge and execution process."

The AI will output a completely new, autonomous Markdown skill file that perfectly replicates the target's output capabilities.
